A Cat's Origin

The first cat species, Felis sylvestris, was from the Near East. All other cats have descended from this species.

A Cat's Home

Cats nowadays find their homes in cities, forests, grasslands, farmlands, tundras, wetlands, and scrublands.

Cats and Civilizations

Cats were loved throughout many ancient civilizations; most notably was Egypt. In Egypt, they symbolized the goddess of love and you could be sentenced to death if you killed a cat.

Domestic and Ferals

With the expansion of cats, however, fewer cats find an actual home. This leads to an increasing amount of ferals: increasing up to 70 million only in the US.
